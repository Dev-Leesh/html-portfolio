Alisha's Top Movie Picks

My top 3 movies of all time are:

Everything Everywhere All at Once

This movie made me aware of how every decision we make will affect the trajectory of our future, no matter how big or small those decisions are.

Armageddon

This movie has such a heartwarming ending about a father's love for his daughter.

Interstellar

Beautiful film. The ending made me wonder about the possibility that this can happen in the real world. I.e, divine intervention.
